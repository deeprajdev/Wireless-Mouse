# Wireless-Mouse
Your Friendly Neighbourhood Trackpad/Mouse.
Turn your phone/tab into a smooth wireless trackpad for your computer.
Scan the QR code, connect on the same Wi-Fi, and control your PC instantly.

---

## Preview

- Real-time wireless connection
- QR based pairing
- Smooth cursor movement
- Left & right click support
- Scroll Up & Scroll Down support
- Works directly from your phone browser

---

## Built With

- JavaScript
- Node.js
- Express
- WebSocket
- Electron
- QRCode

---

## How It Works

1. Launch the desktop app
2. Scan the QR code using your phone
3. Open the generated link
4. Move your finger on the screen
5. Your phone now works as a wireless trackpad

> Both devices should be connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
